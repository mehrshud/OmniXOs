Privacy Policy for OmniXOs

Last Updated: July 14, 2025

omnilertlab ("we," "us," or "our"), under the direction of head developer Mehraban Hamavandy, operates the OmniXOs mobile application (the "Service").

This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our Service and the choices you have associated with that data. By using the Service, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

Information Collection and Use

We collect several different types of information for various purposes to provide and improve our Service to you.

Types of Data Collected

Personal Data You Provide

  • Account Information: When you create an account, we may ask for a username. If you use a third-party login like Google, we receive information from that service, such as your public profile name and email address, which is used solely for account identification.
  • Guest Profile Data: If you play as a guest, we assign a unique guest ID to save your game progress.

Data Collected Automatically

  • Usage Data: We automatically collect information on how the Service is accessed and used. This includes your game progress, scores, leaderboard status, and in-game purchases or interactions.
  • Device Information: We collect information about your mobile device, including the unique device identifier, IP address, operating system, and mobile network information.
  • Advertising ID: We collect your device's advertising identifier (e.g., GAID on Android) to serve personalized advertisements.

How We Use Your Data

  • To provide, maintain, and operate our Service
  • To manage your account and save your game progress
  • To operate the leaderboard and facilitate competition
  • To provide and manage in-app purchases
  • To display personalized and non-personalized advertisements
  • To monitor the usage of our Service and fix technical issues
  • To improve the user experience

Third-Party Service Providers

We use third-party services to facilitate our Service, to provide the Service on our behalf, and to assist us in analyzing how our Service is used. These third parties have access to your Personal Data only to perform these tasks on our behalf and are obligated not to disclose or use it for any other purpose.

  • Google AdMob: Used to display ads within the app. AdMob may collect and use personal data for ad personalization. For more information, please see Google's Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
  • Google Firebase: We use Firebase for backend services, including:
    • Firebase Authentication: To manage user sign-in and accounts.
    • Firebase Realtime Database / Firestore: To store game data like scores and leaderboard information.
    • Firebase Storage: To store user-generated content like profile pictures (if applicable).
    For more information, please see the Firebase Privacy Policy: https://firebase.google.com/policies/analytics
  • Google Play In-App Payments: All in-app purchases are processed through the Google Play Store. We do not collect or store any of your payment card details.

Data Security

The security of your data is important to us, but remember that no method of transmission over the Internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your Personal Data, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

Children's Privacy

Our Service does not address anyone under the age of 13 ("Children"). We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from anyone under the age of 13. If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your Children has provided us with Personal Data, please contact us. If we become aware that we have collected Personal Data from children without verification of parental consent, we take steps to remove that information from our servers.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
