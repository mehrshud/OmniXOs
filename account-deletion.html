Account and Data Deletion for OmniXOs

Last Updated: July 14, 2025

This page provides information regarding the deletion of your account and associated data for the OmniXOs mobile application, operated by omnilertlab.

How to Delete Your Account

You can permanently delete your account and all associated data directly from within the OmniXOs app. Please follow these steps:

  1. Open the OmniXOs app and log in to your account.
  2. Navigate to the 'Profile' or 'Settings' section of the app.
  3. Find and tap the 'Delete Account' button.
  4. You will be asked to confirm your choice. Please be aware that this action is permanent and cannot be undone.

What Data is Deleted?

When you use the in-app delete feature, we will permanently remove the following personal data associated with your account from our Firebase database:

  • Your user account (including any associated email or name from third-party logins like Google Sign-In)
  • Your game progress and scores
  • Your position on the leaderboard

Data We Cannot Delete

Please note that we cannot identify or delete data from anonymous "Guest" profiles, as they are not tied to any personally identifiable information.
